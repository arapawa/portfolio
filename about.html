About

Hi, my name is Josh Braun.

I spend my days doing UX Research and Design, UI Design, and Front-End Web Development.

My background in human-centered research stems from my Bachelor of Science in Anthropology from Central Washington University. I developed skills in ethnography, participant observation, semi-structured interviews, focus groups, and conducting surveys.

In my professional life I've continued to develop my research, design, and programming skills in order to create delightful experiences.

I currently work @ADURO, Inc in lovely Redmond, Washington.

When I'm not working I'm probably playing music, spinning records, or researching game design and development. Like many of us, I'm a big fan of coffee, cookies, and ice cream.
